Our Story

Our History


Founded in 2020, Creators Automated and Engineering Services is a local manufacturer of precision machinery components and machines for industrial setups, including filling machines, mixing machines, mixing tanks, jacketed kettles, conveyors, soap extruders, stamping machines, pipe-laying systems, R.O. systems, water waste disposal units, cosmetic machines, and electrical components.

Today our workshop is located in Antipolo at 500 square meters. We also offer HHO, our new machine line for industrial fuel savings on gasoline, diesel, and LPG.
